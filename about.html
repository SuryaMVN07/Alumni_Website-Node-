About CRRAO AIMSCS

Discover our history, our commitment to research, and the legendary legacy of Prof. C.R. Rao.

The Living Legend: Prof. C.R. Rao

The institute is named after the most celebrated statistician of our times, Padma Vibhushan Prof. C. R. Rao, FRS (1920-2023). The institute stands as a testimony to his ideology of promoting research from grass-root levels and his vision to strengthen the research community in India.

Prestigious Honors

  • International Prize in Statistics (Equivalent to Nobel Prize), 2023
  • National Medal of Science, USA, 2002
  • Padma Vibhushan, Government of India, 2001
